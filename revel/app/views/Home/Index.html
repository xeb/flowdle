Simple

Flowdle lets you enter all your tasks in an easy way. Tasks should be simple descriptions & therefore Flowdle limits you to only 200 characters per task.

Quick

Entering tasks shouldn't break your flow. Flowdle's UI is meant for quickly entering & finding your tasks.

Organized

Sometimes an item on your to-do list crosses over with other things. Instead of creating lists, hierarchies and order, Flowdle lets you enter your tasks with as many or as few tags as you'd like.
