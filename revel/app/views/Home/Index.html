Simple

Flowdle lets you enter your tasks in a concise way. Tasks should be simple descriptions & therefore Flowdle limits you to only 200 characters per task.

Quick

Entering tasks shouldn't break your flow. Flowdle's UI is meant for quickly entering & finding your tasks.

Organized

Sometimes an item on your list crosses over with other things. So why have a list? Instead create, hierarchies and order via tags. Flowdle lets you enter your tasks with as many -- or as few -- tags as you'd like.
